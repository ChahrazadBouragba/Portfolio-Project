About Me

Bridging Functionality and Aesthetics
to Create Engaging and Accessible
Web Applications

Hello! My name is Chahrazad Bouragba. And I am a 23 years old self-taught developer, passionate about creating engaging and dynamic user experiences that combine functionality with aesthetic design.

I strive to deliver high-quality work and approach each task with an open mind and a willingness to learn and grow, allowing me to adapt to each client's unique needs and preferences.

My skill set includes proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and other frontend technologies. I stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices in web development to expand my knowledge and skills.

Services

0.1 Website Development

I specialize in creating visually stunning and responsive websites that are tailored to the needs of each client. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React to build websites from scratch or revamp existing websites to improve their functionality and user experience. I am committed to delivering high-quality work that exceeds my clients' expectations.

0.2 (UI) Design

I offer UI design services to clients, designing interfaces for websites, web applications, and mobile apps. 0.3 Responsive Design

I am skilled at responsive designs that adapt to different screen sizes and resolutions. I use CSS media queries and other techniques to create a consistent user experience across devices. I am committed to ensuring that my clients' websites are accessible and user-friendly on any device, and I work closely with them to understand their needs and goals.
